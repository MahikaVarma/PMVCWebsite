Project Muskaan Video Challenge

The Project Muskaan Video Challenge is a national competition for students to create engaging educational videos in Hindi. Students below the age of 18 can submit original videos on English and Math topics, teaching concepts from grades 6, 7 and 8. The most popular videos have the opportunity to win prizes, so be sure to submit one!

Get Started

About the challenge

Explain a concept from Grade 6,7 or 8 in Hindi using a short video. Contribute to harnessing India's potential and garner the opportunity to win prizes.

Age requirement

Open to all Indian students below the age of 18, however the topic taught must be from Grade 6, 7 or 8.

Topic restriction

Any chapter or topic taught in the local state board or national board from Grades 6, 7 and 8 in english or math.

Language requirements

The primary language used in explaining must be Hindi. Technical terms can be in english.

About Project Muskaan

According to a TRAI telecom (u)statistics report(u) (TRAI, 2019), India has 1,026.37 million active mobile users. Project Muskaan aims to harness this potential and use it to revolutionize India’s educational system.

Project Muskaan is a simple idea. An online social-learning platform with a unique proposition wherein consumers are the producers. Students from all corners of India can create videos teaching concepts they understand in their native languages. This eradicates all language barriers and provides more familiarity than quintessential pedagogical models as concepts are explained by students, for the students.

The students make the videos themselves, using their own vocabulary and explaining concepts from their own perspectives. They can actively interact with each other, asking and answering questions, contributing additional resources and even providing feedback on uploaded videos.

In order to populate such a platform, we have introduced the Project Muskaan Video Challenge. Students can make exciting English and Math videos and submit them to us.
